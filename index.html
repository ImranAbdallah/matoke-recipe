What is Matoke?

Matoke is a unique dish made from green bananas, it is rich in flavors, easy to make and the spices available is a personal choice.It is popular in Uganda, Kenya and Tanzania

Health benefits of Matoke

  1. Matoke is healthy and delicious that can help to loose weight and boost the immune system.
  2. Improve the digestive system, containing only 90 calories per serving, Matoke is filling enough to keep you going all day while being low in calories.
  3. It's also high in fiber, a good source of Potassium and also full of antioxidants.
  4. For people who suffer from Diabetes, Or are at a Risk for Heart disease,Matoke should be included in their diet.
  5. Matoke is an excellent source of vitamin B6 and vitamin C, which eases the absorption of nutrients and minerals.

Ingredients

  • Green bananas.
  • Olive/Sunflower oil, But you can any oil of your choice.
  • Onions.
  • Garlic.
  • Ginger.
  • Red bell Pepper.
  • Tomatoes.
  • Cayenne pepper.
  • Fresh Cilantro.
  • Water.

Steps

  1. Peel the bananas, then cut them into two-inch pieces and place them inside salted water to avoid discoloration when they come into contact with air.
  2. Place a saucepan over medium heat and add the oil to it. Once the oil is hot, add the chopped onions and cook for about 2 minutes then add the garlic,ginger,bell pepper and tomatoes and then cook for about 4 minutes.
  3. Now add in the remaining spices of your choice with the chopped green bananas.
  4. Pour water and stir. Bring the mixture to a boil and cover the pan with a lid. Reduce the heat and let the mixture simmer for 30 minutes or untill the bananas are tender.
  5. Serve with a Salad made of Fresh ripe Avacados,mint,onions and lime.
